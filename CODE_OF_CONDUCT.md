# Code of Conduct

Knead is an early AI product workspace project. We want discussions here to stay useful, respectful, and grounded in the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, direct, and kind.
- Assume good intent, but do not ignore real impact.
- Critique ideas, code, UX, and architecture without attacking people.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on reproducible behavior, product direction, or maintainable implementation.
- Be careful with private data, credentials, user-generated product code, and local workspace files.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or sensitive local files.
- Derailing technical discussions with repeated off-topic arguments.
- Encouraging unsafe handling of provider keys, generated code, or local file access.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the project maintainers privately. For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or take other reasonable actions to keep the project safe and workable.
