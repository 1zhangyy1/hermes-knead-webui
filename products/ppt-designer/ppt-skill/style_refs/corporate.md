# corporate

Professional business style with navy/gold palette — structured, credible, institutional.

## Design Aesthetic

Clean lines, structured layouts, and business-appropriate sophistication. Projects competence, reliability, and institutional credibility. Balances professionalism with approachability through careful whitespace and refined color choices.

## Background

- Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) with navy structural elements
- Texture: None — crisp digital clarity for maximum professionalism

## Typography

### Headlines
Modern geometric sans-serif: "clean, evenly-stroked geometric sans-serif with medium-to-semi-bold weight — contemporary business sensibility, highly legible, no personality extremes. Slightly condensed to feel efficient."

### Body
Humanist sans-serif: "regular-weight humanist sans-serif with open apertures and comfortable letter-spacing — friendly yet professional, optimized for reading comprehension."

## Visual Elements

- Clean charts and data visualizations with labeled axes
- Professional iconography: outlined, consistent stroke weight
- Structured grid layouts with clear column alignment
- Subtle box shadows for card depth (minimal)
- Progress bars and metrics displays
- Organizational charts
- Timeline graphics
- Comparison tables

## Do

- Maintain clear visual hierarchy
- Use consistent grid alignment
- Apply gold accent strategically for emphasis (1–2 elements per slide)
- Keep data visualizations clean and labeled
- Use professional outlined iconography

## Don't

- Use playful or casual elements
- Apply heavy decorative effects or illustrations
- Mix too many accent colors beyond navy/gold
- Crowd slides with information
- Use informal or hand-drawn elements

## Best For

Investor decks, quarterly reports, executive summaries, client proposals, board meetings, corporate communications, consulting deliverables
