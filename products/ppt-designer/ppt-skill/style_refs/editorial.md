# editorial

High-end editorial magazine layout — typography-first, almost no decoration, text IS the design.

## Design Aesthetic

High-end editorial magazine layout. Typography-first, almost no decoration — the text IS the design. Leverages GPT Image 2's strong typography rendering. Think Kinfolk / Apple marketing pages / New York Times feature design. Generous negative space with one confident hero element per slide.

## Background

- Color: Warm Off-White (#FAF8F3) or Near-Black (#121212)
- Texture: None, or very subtle paper texture; very faint gradient wash at most

## Typography

### Headlines
High-contrast serif: "high-contrast display serif with elegant hairline horizontals and thick verticals — extra-large for titles, commanding and literary. Think a premium literary magazine title: refined, confident, not aggressive."

### Body
Clean humanist sans-serif: "regular-weight humanist sans-serif with open apertures and comfortable tracking — recedes behind the headline, professional, never decorative."

Note: Strong weight contrast required — light body text against heavy display headline.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background Light | Warm Off-White | #FAF8F3 | Light mode base |
| Background Dark | Near Black | #121212 | Dark mode base |
| Primary Text | Matched to background | — | Near-black on light, near-white on dark |
| Accent | Single deck color | — | Deep ochre / muted crimson / ink blue / forest green — ONE per deck |

## Visual Elements

- Generous negative space (40%+ of canvas empty)
- Strong hierarchy: one hero element per slide, everything else recedes
- Asymmetric layout, intentional off-center compositions
- Thin hairline rules (1–2px) as the only decoration
- Large numbers as decorative markers ("01", "02")
- Photography or illustration: minimal; if used, monochrome or duotone only

## Do

- Make the primary text enormous and confident
- Use numbers as decorative elements (large "01", "02" markers)
- Embrace asymmetry and off-center compositions
- One accent color for the entire deck, not per slide

## Don't

- No 3D objects, no glassmorphism, no neon glow
- No decorative icons around text
- No drop shadows on text
- No gradient fills on letterforms
- No symmetrical centered layouts
