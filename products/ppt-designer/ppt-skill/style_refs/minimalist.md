# minimalist-business

Clean, calm, corporate-adjacent with taste — McKinsey / Stripe / Linear aesthetic.

## Design Aesthetic

Clean, calm, corporate-adjacent but with taste. McKinsey / Stripe docs / Linear marketing. Trust-inspiring, low-noise, information-dense without being cluttered. Good for consulting, finance, B2B SaaS. Flat design with strong grid discipline.

## Background

- Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) or Very Light Gray (#F7F7F8)
- Texture: None — absolute digital precision

## Typography

### Headlines
Neutral geometric sans-serif, bold: "bold neutral geometric sans-serif with even stroke widths and moderate letter-spacing — authoritative but not aggressive, contemporary business sensibility. Clear 3–4 level hierarchy with small-caps eyebrow text above titles."

### Body
Same family, regular: "regular-weight geometric sans-serif — tabular figures for data pages, comfortable line height. Never decorative, always functional."

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Pure White | #FFFFFF | Main background |
| Alt Background | Light Gray | #F7F7F8 | Section backgrounds |
| Primary Text | Near Black | #0A0A0A | Headlines |
| Body Text | Dark Gray | #2B2B2B | Body text |
| Accent | ONE per deck | — | Corporate blue / forest green / burgundy — for emphasis, section labels, chart highlights only |

## Visual Elements

- Strong grid: 12-column feel, clear column alignment
- Abundant whitespace around every element
- Thin hairline dividers (1px) with section numbers
- Small-caps eyebrow labels above titles
- Small, geometric outlined icons — only when they aid comprehension
- Charts: monochrome with single accent color, fully labeled axes

## Do

- Let whitespace do the work
- Use small-caps eyebrow labels above titles
- Data pages: clean bar/line charts with labeled axes
- Section dividers: thin hairline + section number

## Don't

- No decorative illustrations
- No gradients, no glassmorphism, no 3D
- No emoji, no playful icons
- Don't fill whitespace just because it's empty

## Best For

Consulting deliverables, financial presentations, B2B SaaS pitches, corporate strategy decks, management reporting
