# watercolor

Soft watercolor illustration style — hand-painted textures, natural warmth, artistic.

## Design Aesthetic

Gentle watercolor aesthetic with visible brush strokes and natural color bleeding. Hand-painted feel with soft edges and organic shapes. Warm, approachable, and artistically refined. Combines artistic expression with clear information delivery.

## Background

- Color: Warm Off-White (#FAF8F0) or Soft Cream (#FFF9E6)
- Texture: Subtle watercolor paper texture with visible grain, warm toned

## Typography

### Headlines
Brush script or elegant hand-lettered: "organic brush-lettered display text with natural variation in stroke weight — warm, personal. May appear as actual hand-painted title lettering with visible brush dynamics."

### Body
Casual rounded sans-serif: "clean, slightly rounded sans-serif that maintains artistic cohesion while remaining readable — friendly, not mechanical."

## Visual Elements

- Watercolor washes as section backgrounds
- Illustrated icons with visible brush strokes
- Natural elements: leaves, bubbles, soft botanical shapes
- Color bleeds and soft edges on all elements — no hard geometric lines
- Hand-drawn arrows and connection lines
- Small expressive character illustrations if needed
- Decorative nature accents scattered thoughtfully (not mechanically)

## Do

- Allow color to bleed beyond implied edges
- Use visible brush stroke textures on fills
- Create soft, organic shapes throughout
- Include hand-drawn quality in every element including data charts
- Maintain warm, inviting color palette exclusively

## Don't

- Use sharp geometric shapes (destroys the aesthetic)
- Create hard digital edges or precise outlines
- Use cold or stark colors (cool blue, grey, black backgrounds)
- Add photographic elements
- Make anything look like a corporate diagram

## Best For

Lifestyle content, wellness presentations, travel guides, food and cooking content, personal stories, creative workshops, artistic portfolios, warm educational content, lifestyle brand pitches
