# chinese-elegance

中式美学 — 留白、宋体气质、传统色，文化与文人气息。

## Design Aesthetic

Chinese cultural aesthetics: generous white space (留白), traditional ink-wash influence, refined typographic hierarchy using classical Chinese visual language. Understated, literary, meditative. Every element has intentional placement; negative space is as important as content.

## Background

- Color: 宣纸米白 (#F4EFE6) / Silk Off-White (#EFE9DC) / 淡墨 Deep Ink (#2D3142) for dark variant
- Texture: Very subtle ink-wash texture or clean paper grain — never heavy

## Typography

### Headlines
Song-style typeface visual quality: "high-contrast serif with elegant horizontal strokes and precise vertical strokes — scholarly and refined, like classical Chinese typesetting. Wide-tracked, never condensed. May be set vertically as a decorative element."

### Body
Regular-weight text serif: "regular-weight text serif with comfortable x-height — legible, scholarly, unhurried. Generous line height (1.8+) to breathe."

## Visual Elements

- Very generous white space (≥ 35% of canvas empty)
- 印章 Seal-style stamp as a single decorative accent (vermilion)
- Minimal ink brush lines — sparse, intentional
- Optional vertical dividing lines (朱砂 or 黛青)
- Title positioned top or left, leaving bottom/right to breathe
- Classical balance: asymmetric but harmonious

## Do

- Wide letter-spacing and generous line height throughout
- Only ONE vermilion accent element per slide
- Keep text minimal and poetic — no bullet-point overloading
- Use vertical text as a decorative (not content) element
- Embrace emptiness as the primary design element

## Don't

- Use cartoon illustrations or Western icons
- Apply high-saturation fluorescent colors
- Add modern tech UI elements (cards, badges, progress bars)
- Use condensed or bold-weight Western sans-serif type
- Fill the canvas — empty is the point

## Best For

Cultural institution presentations, traditional arts content, literary and publishing decks, brand decks for Chinese heritage brands, executive presentations needing gravitas and elegance
