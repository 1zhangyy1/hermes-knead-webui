# dark-atmospheric

Dark moody aesthetic with deep colors and glowing accent elements — cinematic, premium, tech.

## Design Aesthetic

Cinematic dark mode aesthetic with atmospheric depth. Deep purples, blacks, and rich shadows with glowing accents creating dramatic visual contrast. Mysterious, sophisticated, and visually striking. Perfect for tech product reveals, creative agencies, and high-impact presentations.

## Background

- Color: Deep Purple-Black (#0D0D1A) or Rich Navy (#1A1A2E)
- Texture: Subtle gradient from darker edges to slightly lighter center, atmospheric depth

## Typography

### Headlines
Elegant light-weight sans-serif on dark: "thin-to-light weight clean sans-serif with generous letter-spacing — high contrast against the dark background, sophisticated. May carry a subtle glow or halo effect at the letterform edges."

### Body
Clean sans-serif in muted light: "regular-weight neutral sans-serif in light gray — readable against dark backgrounds, generous line height, never competing with the glowing accents."

## Visual Elements

- Glowing accent elements and borders (subtle, not garish)
- Subtle gradient backgrounds (edge-to-center depth)
- Atmospheric light orbs and radial glow effects
- Neon-style line highlights on key elements
- Geometric line-work in glow colors
- Cinematic composition: strong focal point, deep margins

## Do

- Maintain high contrast for readability (white/light on dark)
- Use glowing effects sparingly — one or two per slide maximum
- Create atmospheric depth with layered gradients
- Design a dramatic visual focal point per slide
- Keep text crisp and well-spaced against dark backgrounds

## Don't

- Overuse neon effects (less is exponentially more)
- Create low-contrast text combinations (gray on dark gray)
- Use bright or pastel backgrounds
- Add cluttered or busy element arrays
- Mix too many glow colors at once

## Best For

Tech product launches, creative agency pitches, entertainment presentations, gaming content, cinematic storytelling, premium brand reveals, evening events
